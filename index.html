Lorraine Hall Partridge

I'm a junior developer at the Mississippi Coding Academy in Jackson, Mississippi, on the next part of my life's path making a full circle from my beginnings as a NASA intern in the Data Processing Center of the Johnson Space Center. On the way, I've been a roadie, worked in a truck stop, and learned French. I spent three decades changing the world one person at a time in the inner city, bringing the world to my students and empowering them to seek their own horizons.

I'm moving on now, empowering my clients to overcome the challenges on their own paths.

Contact me and we'll seek a unique solution to your challenge.
